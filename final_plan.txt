10-Week MMA Camp — Weekly Execution Plan

Week 1 — Foundation / Structural Restoration

Goal: restore structural tolerance, aerobic support, and technical rhythm before density rises.

Monday — Primary Strength

Weighted Sled Push — 5×15–20 m

Z Press — 4×5

Cross-Body Carry — 3×20–30 m/side

Single-Leg Banded Anti-Extension Hold — 3×20–30 s/side

Rehab

Isometric Spanish Squat (30° Flexion) — 3×20–30 s

Wrist Roller Hold — 3×20–30 s

Neck Massage with Ball (Cervical Line) — 2–3 min

Tuesday — Aerobic Base

Sled Drag (Backward) — 8×20–30 m at smooth pace

Nasal Breathing Walk Tempo — 15–20 min

Breathing Protocol Deep Work — 5–8 min

Wednesday — Secondary Strength

Dumbbell Alternating Incline Press — 3×8

Bent-Over Row — 3×8–10

Sandbag Bear Hug Carry — 3×20 m

Slow Tempo Eccentrics on Major Lifts — 2–3 controlled sets on first lower-body lift

Rehab

Open-Close Grip Ball — 2×20–30 reps

Neuro-Friendly Band Activation — 2 rounds

Compression Wrap with Mobility Flow — 5 min

Thursday — Alactic Coordination

Reactive shot or scramble bursts — 6–8×6–8 s

Rest 60–90 s between reps

Stop if speed or position quality drops

Friday — Controlled Repeatability

Light positional chain work — 6×45 s / 75 s rest

Keep effort submaximal and movement clean

This is not the hard fight-pace day

Saturday — Recovery / Tissue Care

Breathwork + Parasympathetic Ground Flow — 8–10 min

Limb Elevation + Isometric Squeeze — 2–3 rounds

Easy mobility flow — 10–15 min

Week 2 — Build / Repeatability

Keep the exact weekly structure from Week 1.

Adjustments

Weighted Sled Push or Z Press: add 1 set only if freshness stays good

Sled Drag (Backward) or Nasal Breathing Walk Tempo: add 5 minutes total work

Thursday alactic work: add 1 rep only if speed stays sharp

If wrestling quality drops, remove the added volume immediately

Week 3 — Build / Repeatability

Keep the exact weekly structure from Week 2.

Adjustments

Slight load increase on Monday and Wednesday main lifts

Aerobic work stays smooth and nasal-dominant

Friday remains controlled; do not turn it into a hard glycolytic session

Week 4 — Bridge / Transfer

Work becomes more MMA-transfer oriented while keeping the base alive.

Monday — Strength + Transfer

Weighted Sled Push — 4×15–20 m

Z Press — 3×5–6

Sandbag Carry — 3×20 m

Single-Leg Rotational Hold — 3×10–20 s/side

Rehab

Isometric Pain-Free Loading (Top/Bottom Range) — 2–3 sets

Wrist Roller Hold — 2–3 sets

Neck Massage with Ball (Cervical Line) — 2 min

Tuesday — Aerobic Support

Light Sled Push — 8×15–20 m

Nasal Breathing Walk Tempo — 15–20 min

Mobility flow

Wednesday — Secondary Strength

Bent-Over Row — 3×8

Trap Bar Jump or High Pull — 4×3

Split-Stance Anti-Rotation Hold — 3×20 s/side

Neuro-Friendly Band Activation — 2 rounds

Thursday — Alactic Coordination

Shot-entry or footwork bursts — 6×6 s

Full recovery between reps

Friday — Controlled Repeatability

Positional chain intervals — 6–8×45 s / 75 s rest

Keep takedown speed and mat quality clean

Saturday — Recovery

Breathwork + Parasympathetic Ground Flow

Open-Close Grip Ball

Compression Wrap with Mobility Flow

Week 5 — Specific Entry

Fight-specific work begins.

Monday — Specific Strength / Power

Front Squat — 4×4

Medicine Ball Rotational Slam — 4×3/side

Banded Row (speed focus) — 4×5

Barbell Rollout — 3×6–8

Rehab

Isometric Dumbbell Wrist Hold (Neutral) — 3×20–30 s

Isometric Spanish Squat (30° Flexion) — 3×20–30 s

Multi-Plane Neck Holds — 2 rounds

Tuesday — Alactic Sharpness

Explosive shot, sprawl, or strike-entry bursts — 6–8×6–10 s

Rest 90 s

Wednesday — Secondary Strength

High Pull — 4×3

Explosive Incline Press — 3×5

Sandbag Carry — 3×20 m

Forearm Supination/Pronation Paused — 2–3 sets

Thursday — Aerobic Rhythm

Tempo shadow grappling or footwork — 15–20 min

Breathing Protocol Deep Work — 5 min

Keep this easy

Friday — Fight-Pace Density

Grapple Circuits (High Pace) — 5×3 min

90 s rest

If sparring or live goes are heavy this week, reduce this slot

Saturday — Recovery

Pronation/Supination with Band — 2–3 sets

Scap-Neck Coordination Drill — 2 rounds

Light aerobic recovery and mobility

Week 6 — Specific Density Build

Keep the structure from Week 5.

Adjustments

Monday Front Squat slightly heavier if knee feels calm

Friday Grapple Circuits (High Pace) can become 6×3 min only if live work is not already hard

Tuesday alactic work stays sharp, not fatiguing

If takedown speed or hand-fighting quality drops, reduce Friday first

Week 7 — Specific Density Build

Same structure as Week 6.

Progression

Monday strength stays strong but crisp

Wednesday remains explosive, not grinding

Friday can use Banded Sled Push (Light) after the main circuit for 4–6×15–20 m only if recovery is good

Week 8 — Specific Density Build

Same structure as Week 7, but start trimming anything non-essential.

Key Rule

Keep Front Squat, Grapple Circuits (High Pace), alactic sharpness, and rehab

Cut extra accessory volume before cutting the main work

Mindset Cues

GPP

Build clean work capacity without chasing fatigue.

Win the session by staying repeatable.

SPP

Make fight pace feel normal.

Sharp execution first, ego second.

Taper

Do less, but do it clean.
