## PHASE 1: GENERAL PREPARATION PHASE (GPP) - 2 WEEKS (14 DAYS)

### Mindset Focus

- **Generic:** Pick a simple daily intention and review one win after each session.

### Strength & Power

**Phase:** GPP
**Weekly Progression:** Add 1 set or ~5–10% load weekly; deload final week by ~20%.
**If Time Short:** Keep top 2 lifts + 1 trunk/neck drill.

**Top Exercises:** Pallof Press; Single-Arm Chest Press (Floor); Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift Hold; Suitcase Carry Holds; Cross-Body Carry; Seated Medicine Ball Rotation; Russian Twist (Weighted); Single-Leg Banded Anti-Extension Hold; 🔥 Single-Leg Rotational Med Ball Hold; DB Split Squat; Neck Harness Isometrics; TRX-Assisted Squat; Single-Leg RDL (light)

**Prescriptions by Exercise Type:**
- **Ballistics (Med Ball / Speed / Power):** 3–5x4–6 reps (or 6–10 throws) at crisp intent; rest 60–90s.
- **Isometrics:** 3–5 holds x 10–20s @ 7–9/10 effort; full rest between holds.
- **Core Control:** 2-4 sets x 6-10 reps or 20-40s tempo (3-1-3), RPE 6-8.
- **General Strength:** 2–3x6–10 @ RPE 6–7, keep reps crisp.

**Weekly Progression:** Add 1 round or ~5–10% volume weekly; deload final week by ~20%.
**If Time Short:** Keep 2 aerobic rounds + 1 alactic pop.
**If Fatigue High:** If fatigue high: drop 1–2 rounds, keep intensity easy.

## PHASE 2: SPECIFIC PREPARATION PHASE (SPP) - 2 WEEKS (14 DAYS)

### Mindset Focus

- **Generic:** Train with a clear performance cue and keep the focus on execution quality.

### Strength & Power

**Phase:** SPP
**Weekly Progression:** Hold volume, increase intensity or bar speed weekly; deload final week by ~20%.
**If Time Short:** Keep heavy lift + paired explosive + trunk.

**Top Exercises:** Pallof Press; Med-Ball Rotational Slam; Band-Resisted Punch; Banded Row (Speed Focus); Overhead Slam (Rotational); Farmer Carry (Heavy)

**Prescriptions by Exercise Type:**
- **Ballistics (Med Ball / Speed / Power):** 4–6x2–5 reps at max speed; full rest 60–120s.
- **Core Control:** 2-4 sets x 6-10 reps or 20-40s tempo (3-1-3), RPE 6-8.

### Conditioning

#### Conditioning Block SPP — Aerobic Base + Glycolytic Power + Alactic Speed + Fight-Pace Density
**Intent:** Match fight demands with intervals and skill-relevant density.
**Dosage Template:** 4–6 rounds of 2–5 min @ RPE 7–8, work:rest 1:1–1:0.5 (cap 18–25 min). Template applies unless a drill lists its own structure.
**Weekly Progression:** Increase density or intensity weekly; keep volume flat; deload final week by ~20%.
**If Time Short:** Keep 2 fight-pace rounds (system priority).
**If Fatigue High:** If fatigue high: drop volume, keep rest longer.

**System: AEROBIC** (scaled by format emphasis)
- **Drill: Lateral Shuffle + Strike Drills**  • Load: low<br>  • Rest: —<br>  • Timing: 3 x 5min rounds<br>  • Purpose: Emphasize angles, not explosiveness (light)<br>  • Red Flags: None

**System: GLYCOLYTIC** (scaled by format emphasis)
- **Drill: Roll Under Hell (medicine_ball)**  • Load: high (10-15lb med ball)<br>  • Rest: —<br>  • Timing: 20 roll-under uppercuts, 10 body shots x 5 rounds<br>  • Purpose: Simulate rolling under hooks to counter (85% power)<br>  • Red Flags: None

**System: ALACTIC** (scaled by format emphasis)
- **Drill: Explosive Boxing Burst Intervals**  • Load: RPE 8–9, keep quality high and stop before speed drop-off<br>  • Rest: 75-120 sec complete rest between reps<br>  • Timing: 6–8 x 6–10 sec fast punch bursts<br>  • Purpose: Minimal-dose ATP-PCr maintenance for striking speed and neural sharpness.<br>  • Red Flags: Terminate set if punch speed/position quality drops.

#### Conditioning Block SPP — Glycolytic Power + Fight-Pace Density
**Intent:** Match fight demands with intervals and skill-relevant density.
**Dosage Template:** 4–6 rounds of 2–5 min @ RPE 7–8, work:rest 1:1–1:0.5 (cap 18–25 min). Template applies unless a drill lists its own structure.
**Weekly Progression:** Increase density or intensity weekly; keep volume flat; deload final week by ~20%.
**If Time Short:** Keep 2 fight-pace rounds (system priority).
**If Fatigue High:** If fatigue high: drop volume, keep rest longer.

**System: GLYCOLYTIC** (scaled by format emphasis)
- **Drill: Philly Shell Torture (bands, dumbbells)**  • Load: high (5lb DBs in lead hand)<br>  • Rest: —<br>  • Timing: 1min band-resisted shoulder rolls, 30s counters x 6<br>  • Purpose: Maintain defensive posture while countering (80% effort)<br>  • Red Flags: None

- **Drill: Medicine Ball Pass Intervals (medicine_ball)**  • Load: high<br>  • Rest: —<br>  • Timing: 6x30s hard passes, 30s rest<br>  • Purpose: Rotational upper body work without impact<br>  • Red Flags: None

## PHASE 3: TAPER - 2 WEEKS (14 DAYS)

### Mindset Focus

- **Generic:** Keep cues simple, visualize the opening exchange, and stay calm.

### Strength & Power

**Phase:** TAPER
**Weekly Progression:** Cut total volume 40–60%, keep intensity crisp; last 3–5 days very light.
**If Time Short:** Keep 1 neural primer + 1 trunk/neck drill.

**Top Exercises:** Anti-Rotation Med Ball Slam; Band-Resisted Push Press; Medicine-Ball Chest Toss; Deadbug (Band-Resisted)

**Prescriptions by Exercise Type:**
- **Ballistics (Med Ball / Speed / Power):** 3–5x2–4 reps/throws at max speed; full rest 60–120s.
- **Core Control:** 2-4 sets x 6-10 reps or 20-40s tempo (3-1-3), RPE 6-8.

### Conditioning

AEROBIC (Status: Not prescribed)
Reason: Tapering; volume reduced to preserve freshness.
Coach option: Optional 1 early-week exposure at 60–70% volume if athlete needs confidence.

**Dosage Template:** 6–10 rounds of 6–12 sec @ RPE 8–9, rest 60–120 sec (cap 8–12 min). Template applies unless a drill lists its own structure.
**Weekly Progression:** Reduce volume 40–60%; keep speed sharp; last 3–5 days very light.
**If Time Short:** Keep 4–6 alactic bursts + shadowboxing rhythm.
**If Fatigue High:** If fatigue high: keep only 4–6 low-impact bursts.

**Core Recovery Strategies:**
- Daily breathwork (5–10 mins post-session)
- Optional contrast shower: 1 min hot / 1 min cold × 5 (comfort-based; avoid if it disrupts sleep).

- Cold exposure 2–3x/week (if needed)
- Mobility circuits/light recovery work daily

**GPP Recovery Focus:**
- Focus on tissue prep, joint mobility
- Reset sleep routine
