## Nutrition

🍽️ **Nutrition Module**
- 3 core meals + 2–3 snacks daily
- Whole foods focus: lean protein, complex carbs, healthy fats
- Protein intake: 1.7–2.2 g/kg → 134.3–173.8 g/day
- Hydration: 0.03–0.04 l/kg → 2370.0–3160.0 ml/day

**SPP Phase Focus:**
- Moderate calorie deficit or maintenance for lean conditioning
- Carbohydrates: 3–6 g/kg (focus on 4–6 g/kg around sessions) → 237.0–474.0 g/day
- Protein: 1.6–2.2 g/kg → 126.4–173.8 g/day
- Fats: 0.7–1.0 g/kg (20–25% calories) → 55.3–79.0 g/day

**High Fatigue in SPP:**
- Maintain calories at maintenance, prioritize carbs around sessions
- Continue intra-workout fueling 30–60 g carbs/hour
- Magnesium glycinate 300 mg + taurine 1.5 g (evening)
- Electrolytes during and post-training

**Meal Timing Guidelines:**
- Pre-training: 1.5–3h before, balanced meal with 1–2 g/kg carbs + ~0.3 g/kg protein
- Intra-training (>60 min): 30–60 g carbs/hour (sports drinks/gels), hydration
- Post-training (within 1h): 1–1.2 g/kg carbs + 0.3–0.4 g/kg protein

**⚠️ Weight Cut Protocol Triggered:**
- Weight cut > 6.8%: aggressive refeed post-weigh-in
- Carbs: 8–12 g/kg over recovery period if heavy cut, else 4–7 g/kg
- Multiple meals/snacks with high-GI carbs (rice, pasta, sports drinks)
- Protein: 0.3–0.4 g/kg per feeding (lean meat, whey)
- Avoid high fat/fiber first hours post-weigh-in
- Hydrate aggressively: initial bolus 0.6–0.9 L + replace 150% fluid lost
- Sodium intake: 20–50+ mmol/L drinks + salted snacks/broths
- De-emphasize diuretics (caffeine/alcohol) final 24h pre-fight
- Alkaline buffer (sodium bicarbonate ~0.3 g/kg) 90–120 min pre-fight if tolerated
- Final carb snack 1–2 h pre-fight: 1–2 g/kg easily digested carbs

## Recovery

**Core Recovery Strategies:**
- Daily breathwork (5–10 mins post-session)
- Optional contrast shower: 1 min hot / 1 min cold × 5 (comfort-based; avoid if it disrupts sleep).
- 8–9 hours of sleep/night + 90-min blue light cutoff
- Cold exposure 2–3x/week (if needed)
- Mobility circuits/light recovery work daily

**Fatigue Red Flags:**
- Drop 1 session if sleep < 6.5hrs for 3+ days
- Cut weekly volume by 25–40%
- Replace eccentrics with isometrics if DOMS >72hrs
- Monitor for appetite/mood dips (cortisol/motivation risk)

**SPP Recovery Focus:**
- Manage CNS load and alactic fatigue
- Introduce 1–2 full recovery days

**⚠️ Severe Weight Cut Recovery Warning:**
- Cut >6% → elevate recovery urgency
- Add 2 float tank or Epsom salt baths in fight week
- Emphasize post-weigh-in refeed: fluids, high-GI carbs
- Monitor mood, sleep, and hydration hourly post-weigh-in
- Consider medical supervision if possible
